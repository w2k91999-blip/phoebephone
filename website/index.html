FAQs

Questions, answered

Will callers know it's an AI?

Phoebe sounds warm and natural, and most callers simply feel well looked after. You can choose whether she introduces herself as a virtual assistant — it's your call.

Do I have to change my phone number?

No. You keep your existing number and simply forward calls to Phoebe whenever you want her to answer — all the time, after hours, or only when you're busy.

How long does setup take?

Most businesses are live in under a minute. You tell Phoebe about your services, prices and hours, and she's ready to take calls.

What does Phoebe capture on a call?

The caller's name, the reason they're ringing, their location and any booking details — sent to you instantly by text and email, with a full transcript.

Is it really unlimited minutes?

Yes. Every plan includes unlimited call minutes — no per-minute charges and no contracts. You can cancel any time.

Which businesses is Phoebe good for?

Any local service business that takes bookings or enquiries by phone — from driving instructors and salons to tradespeople and clinics.
