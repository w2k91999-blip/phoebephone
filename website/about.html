About Phone Phoebe

Built for businesses
that run on phone calls

We built Phoebe because small businesses in the UK were losing customers every time their phone rang and went unanswered. There had to be a better way.

The problem we're solving

Every day, UK tradespeople, driving instructors, salon owners and local professionals miss calls — not because they don't care, but because they're busy doing the actual work. They're mid-lesson, up a ladder, or with a client in the chair.

Most callers won't leave a voicemail. They'll just ring the next business in the Google results. That's a lost booking, a lost customer, and money that never shows up in your bank account.

A human receptionist costs £20,000+ a year. An answering service is impersonal and misses the nuance of your business. And both still leave you with the admin of calling back.

What Phoebe does

Phoebe is a warm, natural-sounding AI receptionist that answers your calls when you can't. She knows your services, your prices, your hours, and your most common questions — because you tell her. She answers in your business's name, books straight into your calendar, and texts you the details the moment the call ends.

You keep your existing number. Setup takes under a minute. And from £39 a month, she costs less per day than a cup of coffee.

Who we help

Phone Phoebe was built first for driving instructors — there are over 39,000 approved driving instructors in the UK, and almost all of them run their business alone. But Phoebe works for any local service business that takes bookings or enquiries by phone:

  • Driving instructors
  • Salons and barbers
  • Tradespeople (plumbers, electricians, builders)
  • Cleaners and gardeners
  • Clinics, dentists and physios
  • Solicitors and accountants
  • Removal companies and estate agents

If your phone rings and you can't always answer it, Phoebe is for you.

Who we are

Phone Phoebe was founded by Chris Corkhill, a UK-based entrepreneur who has spent years working alongside small business owners. Chris built the product after watching too many hard-working people lose business to a ringing phone they couldn't pick up.

We're a small, focused team. We ship fast, we listen to our customers, and we measure success by the bookings Phoebe wins for the businesses she serves.
