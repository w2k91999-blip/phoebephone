## CONTENT PILLARS (the 4 topics everything fits under)

Every post must serve one of these four pillars. This keeps the feed coherent and builds trust over time.

| Pillar | Purpose | % of content |
|--------|---------|--------------|
| **Pain** | Surface the missed-call problem. Make it real and costly. | 35% |
| **Proof** | Show Phoebe working. Real calls, real bookings, real results. | 30% |
| **Education** | Teach something useful. Builds authority and earns shares. | 20% |
| **Personality** | Behind-the-scenes, founder POV, relatable moments. | 15% |

---

## PLATFORM STRATEGY

### Facebook (Primary — where UK driving instructors actually are)
- Age 30–58 skews heavily to Facebook, not TikTok
- Best format: Native video (uploaded directly, not YouTube link) + text posts
- Best times: 7–9am, 12–1pm, 5–7pm weekdays
- Groups strategy: Join UK ADI groups and post value-first content (not ads)
  - "ADI News UK" (Facebook Group)
  - "UK Driving Instructors" (Facebook Group)
  - "Driving Instructor Forum" (Facebook Group)
  - Rule: never hard-sell. Post the education content. Let people ask.

### Instagram (Secondary — visual proof and brand building)
- Best format: Reels (3× organic reach vs static), then carousels
- Best times: Tue–Thu, 8–10am or 6–8pm
- Stories: Use for polls ("Do you miss calls while teaching?") and quick tips
- Highlights: Create a "How it works" and "Real calls" highlight

### TikTok (Long-term play — low priority until video is ready)
- Younger audience but useful for brand awareness with PDIs starting out
- Activate only when you have a real Phoebe call recording to show
- Algorithm rewards watch time and replays — hooks must be in first 1.5s

### LinkedIn (Niche but high-value)
- Targets ADI trainers, franchise owners, fleet managers — higher ticket
- Long-form personal story posts get disproportionate reach
- 1–2 posts per week max

## ENGAGEMENT TACTICS

- Reply to every comment within 2 hours for the first 48 hours (algorithm rewards early engagement)
- Ask a question at the end of every caption — creates comments, which boost reach
- Pin your best-performing post (usually the "maths" or "I tested this" video)
- Use Instagram Stories polls weekly: "Do you miss calls while teaching?" (Yes / More than I'd like)
- Respond to every DM personally — at this stage each lead is precious
