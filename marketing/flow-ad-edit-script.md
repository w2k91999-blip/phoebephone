# Phone Phoebe — Flow Ad Edit Script
## For the 3 Veo/Google Flow clips, assembled into one ad (~30s)

This is the assembly guide. Generate the 3 clips in Flow, then drop them into
CapCut (free) in this order and lay the voiceover + on-screen text over the top.

---

## STRUCTURE AT A GLANCE

| Section | Clip | Time | Job |
|---------|------|------|-----|
| Hook | Clip A (phone buzzing) | 0–6s | Stop the scroll — name the pain |
| Demo | Clip B (Phoebe answering) | 6–18s | Show the fix working |
| Payoff | Clip C (booking confirmed) | 18–25s | Show the happy outcome |
| CTA | Static end card | 25–30s | Risk-free call to action |

Total: ~30 seconds. Vertical 9:16.

---

## FULL SCRIPT (voiceover + on-screen text, second by second)

### 🎬 SECTION 1 — HOOK (Clip A: hands on wheel, phone buzzing)

**On-screen text (big, top third):**
> Every missed call is a missed booking.

**Voiceover (warm, calm British voice):**
> "You're mid-lesson. Your phone rings. You can't answer it."

**Timing:** 0–6s
**Note:** Let the phone-buzzing visual breathe. Text appears at 0.5s. VO starts at 1s.

---

### 🎬 SECTION 2 — DEMO (Clip B: Phoebe's voice answering)

**On-screen text (lower third, as a caption):**
> Phoebe answers for you.

**Voiceover continues:**
> "So Phoebe answers it for you. She sounds real, knows your prices, and books the lesson — while you keep teaching."

**Phoebe's voice (overlaid clip audio or your ElevenLabs clip):**
> "Hi there! I've got Tuesday at 2pm free — shall I book that in for you?"

**Timing:** 6–18s
**Note:** This is the heart of the ad. If Clip B's generated voice is good, use it. If not, mute the clip and drop in your ElevenLabs Phoebe audio.

---

### 🎬 SECTION 3 — PAYOFF (Clip C: booking confirmed, happy instructor)

**On-screen text (animated, centre):**
> ✅ New booking confirmed — Tuesday 2pm

**Voiceover:**
> "You get a text with all the details. No missed calls. No lost students."

**Timing:** 18–25s
**Note:** Sync the "✅ New booking confirmed" text to land exactly when she smiles at her phone.

---

### 🎬 SECTION 4 — CTA END CARD (static — make in Canva or reuse our card)

**On-screen text:**
> **Never miss a booking again.**
>
> Try Phoebe FREE for 7 days
> No card · Keep your number · Cancel any time
>
> 📞 phonephoebe.co.uk

**Voiceover:**
> "Phone Phoebe. Try her free for seven days."

**Timing:** 25–30s
**Note:** Hold this card for a full 4–5 seconds so the URL sticks. Use the brand navy + purple.

---

## VOICEOVER — FULL SCRIPT (clean, for recording in one take)

Record this in ElevenLabs (British female, e.g. Lily) or read it yourself:

> "You're mid-lesson. Your phone rings. You can't answer it.
> So Phoebe answers it for you. She sounds real, knows your prices, and books the lesson — while you keep teaching.
> You get a text with all the details. No missed calls. No lost students.
> Phone Phoebe. Try her free for seven days."

**Length when spoken:** ~22 seconds — fits perfectly across the 30s edit with natural pauses.

## CAPTIONS NOTE

Turn on **Auto Captions** in CapCut for the voiceover too — even with the text
overlays, full subtitles boost watch time on mute. Style them white with a thin
black outline, centred, lower third.

---

## MUSIC SUGGESTIONS (CapCut free library — search these moods)

- "Corporate uplifting" — clean, trustworthy
- "Soft inspiring" — warm, human
- Keep it at **10–15% volume** under the voiceover — it should be felt, not heard.
