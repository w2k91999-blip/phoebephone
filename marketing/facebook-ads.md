# Phone Phoebe — Facebook & Instagram Paid Ads
## Audience: UK Driving Instructors | Budget: £200/month | Objective: Trial sign-ups

---

## STRATEGIC OVERVIEW

With £200/month you cannot win on volume — you win on **precision targeting + conversion psychology**.
The goal is not clicks. The goal is free trial starts that convert to £39/month subscribers.

**Core psychological levers for driving instructors:**
1. **Loss aversion** — they're already losing money; make that real and quantified
2. **Identity** — ADIs are proud professionals; talk to them as experts, not victims
3. **Simplicity** — they're not tech buyers; if it sounds complicated they won't try it
4. **Trust** — new product, no reviews yet; use specificity as a proxy for social proof

---

│

## CREATIVE A — "THE MISSED CALL" (Pain-led / Loss Aversion)

**Best for:** Cold audiences who don't know Phoebe exists
**Psychological trigger:** Loss aversion (losses hurt 2× more than equivalent gains)

**Primary text (mobile-first — hook in line 1):**
> While you were teaching your last lesson, someone called you.
>
> They didn't leave a message.
>
> They rang the next instructor. They're booked in now.
>
> This happens 3–5 times a week for the average driving instructor. At £38 a lesson, that's up to £190 a week — quietly disappearing.
>
> Phone Phoebe answers every call you miss. She sounds natural, answers their questions and books the lesson straight into your calendar. You get a text with the details.
>
> 7-day free trial. No card. Keep your existing number.

**Headline:** You just lost another booking
**Description:** From £39/month. Set up in 60 seconds.
**CTA:** Sign Up

**Visual direction:**
- Split-screen: Left = instructor at wheel, phone face-down; Right = phone screen showing "New booking: Tue 2pm — Sarah T."
- Overlay text: "3 missed calls while you were teaching" (white on dark)
- NO stock photo faces — use device screens and hands only (performs better for trades audiences)
- Aspect ratio: 4:5 for Feed, 9:16 for Reels

## LEAD FORM SETUP

**Form type:** Higher intent (not "more volume" — filter for quality)
**Headline:** Try Phoebe free for 7 days
**Intro:** "Tell us a bit about your business and we'll get Phoebe set up for you today."

**Questions (keep to 3 max):**
1. "How many calls do you estimate you miss per week?" (1–2 / 3–5 / 5+ / Not sure)
2. "Do you currently use a booking system?" (Yes — Cal.com / Yes — other / No)
3. "When would you like Phoebe to start?" (As soon as possible / Within a week / Just exploring)

**Privacy note:** Add "We'll never share your details. Unsubscribe any time." — required for GDPR and boosts conversion.

**Thank-you screen:**
> "You're in. We'll send setup instructions to your email within the hour.
> While you wait — call our demo line and hear exactly how Phoebe sounds: [number]"

## BUDGET ALLOCATION & OPTIMISATION SCHEDULE

**Week 1–2:** Run all 3 cold creatives equally. DO NOT TOUCH. Let Meta learn.
**Week 3:** Pause the lowest CTR creative. Double budget on the winner.
**Week 4:** Launch retargeting (Ad Set 2) if pixel has 100+ website visitors.
**Month 2:** Launch Lookalike (Ad Set 3) once you have 50+ lead form submissions.

**KPIs to watch:**
| Metric | Target | Action if missed |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| CTR (link) | >1.5% | New hook/visual |
| Cost per lead | <£5 | Tighten audience |
| Lead-to-trial rate | >40% | Fix thank-you email |
| Trial-to-paid rate | >25% | Fix nurture sequence |

**The pixel is non-negotiable.** Install it on phonephoebe.co.uk before spending a penny. Every visitor is future retargeting gold.
Instructions: Meta Business Suite → Events Manager → Connect Data Source → Web → Get Pixel Code → paste in `<head>` of index.html.

## COPY RULES (apply to every ad)

- **Never say "AI"** in the first sentence — leads with the outcome, not the technology
- **Lead with loss, close with hope** — pain first, then the easy fix
- **One CTA only** — never give two options in the same ad
- **Numbers beat adjectives** — "£152/week lost" beats "significant lost revenue"
- **Short paragraphs** — 1–2 sentences max per block. Mobile readers skim.
- **Emoji sparingly** — 1–2 max, only to replace a bullet point or draw the eye
