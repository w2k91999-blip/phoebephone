## STRATEGY

The free trial is won or lost in the first 48 hours.

If someone sets Phoebe up and she answers a real call — they'll pay. Every time.
If they never get her set up, they'll cancel without ever experiencing the product.

This sequence has one job: **get Phoebe answering a real call before Day 3.**

**Platform:** Any email tool works — Mailchimp (free tier), Brevo (free), ConvertKit
**Trigger:** Immediately on lead form submission or trial sign-up
**Send time:** All emails sent between 7–9am UK time (highest open rates for tradespeople)
